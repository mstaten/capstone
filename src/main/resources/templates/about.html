Local Reports is a web app that allows you to share valuable information about the state of local streets. For a problem anywhere from potholes to flooded roads, you can report it in detail and pinpoint its location on the map. You can also view all reports submitted by others and their locations on the map so you know whether an issue has been reported yet.

Created by drivers like you who are sick of dodging that one pothole that never seems to get filled in.

By people experiencing power outages who want to know whether homes around them are affected as well.

And by commuters just trying to get home from work who need to know if there is flooding along their route home.

Here's to you!

Steps to submit a report:
  1. Enter your location in the search bar
  2. Drop a pin
  3. Fill out report title & description
  4. Select the level of urgency
  5. Hit Submit!
Issues you might report:
  • pothole
  • tree blocking a street
  • damaged or missing street sign
  • flooded roads
  • excessive trash
  • power outage
  • and so on!
